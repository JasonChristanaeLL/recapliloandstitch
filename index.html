Lilo & Stitch

  • Animated: 2002, Walt Disney Animation Studios
  • Live Action: 2022, Disney+
  • Genre: Animation / Live Action, Family, Comedy
  • Setting: Hawaii, Earth & Galactic Federation

Lilo & Stitch follows Experiment 626, a genetically engineered alien created only for destruction. After escaping intergalactic prison, he crash-lands in Hawaii and is adopted by a lonely girl named Lilo. While Lilo struggles to hold her small family together with her sister Nani, Stitch slowly learns the true meaning of togetherness through the iconic message of “Ohana”—family that never leaves anyone behind. It marks Stitch’s journey from a wild creature into someone capable of caring and belonging.
